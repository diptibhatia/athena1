Bio

Firoz leads the group wide businesses ensuring that a strong commitment to high standards and a performance culture are embedded across the entire group business. Coming from a BFSI background with strong management credentials, he has led Westford from very humble beginnings in 2008 from a small training academy to an international network of business schools having students from 40 nationalities across the globe. During his last position in the capacity of Country Head - Retail Banking Sales for a large bank in UAE, Firoz was driven by his vision to engage in the education industry. His keen intent to contribute in the rapid transformation of the education industry, which had started to leverage technology to disrupt the existing models of education making it more accessible and affordable. Firoz holds dual MBA's, first from the University of Wales, UK (Marketing) and second from TIU, Missouri, US (Banking & Finance)
